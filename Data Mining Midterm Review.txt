Data Mining Midterm Review:

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

-ML Models-

Two main types:

-Supervised models, two sub types:

	-Classification, makes discrete predictions (aka labels a target feature)

	-Regression, makes contiuous predictions (aka price predictions)

-Unsupervised learning

	-Clustering, K-Nearest Neighboor (KNN)

Biggest difference

-Supervised models have a clear goal and generally implement explicit rules, whereas unsupervised models do not have such clear goals and instead aim to learn the rules implicitly through the data.
